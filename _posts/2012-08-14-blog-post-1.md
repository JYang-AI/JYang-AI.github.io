Despite the great success of AI in the areas of vision, language, robotics, computer systems, etc, we believe machine learning can be used in broader areas of engineering, nature and even social science. Problems in many areas are solved by domain knowledge like approximated models and euqations. 

Machine learning has the potential to be applied in broader domains of engineering, science and social goods. Typically, domain knowledge like models and equations exist in these areas. We aim to develop several genral learning frameworks driven by domain-specific knowledge and data.
